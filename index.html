FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Will the AI Product Manager touch my store?
No. It ships in shadow mode: playbooks queue recommendations and every one waits for your explicit approval. It executes nothing — and execution stays off until its tracked hit rate earns your trust.
Is my store data safe?
Your file is parsed and your numbers are computed in your browser. When you use AI insights or save a report, the data needed is sent over an encrypted connection and stored in your account until you delete it. Your data is never sold or used to train our models.
What exports do you support?
Standard order exports from Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Amazon Seller Central. If your platform exports orders as CSV, it almost certainly works.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — one click from your billing page. Your plan stays active until the end of the period you paid for, and your reports stay exportable.
How is margin actually computed?
Revenue − COGS − platform fees − allocated ad cost, per SKU, per period — executed as real pandas against your export. Click any figure and ProfitFalcon shows the code that produced it.
