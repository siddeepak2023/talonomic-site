FAQ

Before you ask.

Will the AI Product Manager touch my store?
No. It ships in shadow mode: playbooks queue recommendations and every one waits for your explicit approval. It executes nothing — and execution stays off until its tracked hit rate earns your trust.
Does my order data leave my browser?
Computation runs in a WebAssembly Python runtime in your browser. The AI sees your questions and summary context, not your raw customer rows.
What exports do you support?
Standard order exports from Shopify, WooCommerce, and Amazon Seller Central. If your platform exports orders as CSV, it almost certainly works.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — one click from your billing page. Your plan stays active until the end of the period you paid for, and your reports stay exportable.
How is margin actually computed?
Revenue − COGS − platform fees − allocated ad cost, per SKU, per period — executed as real pandas against your export. Click any figure and Talonomic shows the code that produced it.
