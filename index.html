FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install a pixel or connect my store? +

No. Talonomic reads a plain order export — CSV, Excel, or a Google Sheet. There’s no pixel, no OAuth, and no developer setup. Export your orders and drop the file in.

Is my store data safe? +

Your file is parsed and your numbers computed in your browser. When you use AI insights or save a report, the data needed is sent over an encrypted connection and stored in your account until you delete it. We never sell your data or use it to train our own models.

How is this different from attribution tools? +

Attribution tools focus on where sales came from. Talonomic is profit-first: it computes your real unit economics — margin, LTV, per-SKU profit — from a file you already have, in 60 seconds, from $20/mo.

Which platforms work? +

Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Amazon exports all work, plus any CSV, Excel file, or Google Sheet. Talonomic auto-detects your columns.
