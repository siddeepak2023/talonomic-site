Talonomic vs Triple Whale vs Polar Analytics

Different tools built around different questions. Triple Whale and Polar are strong connector dashboards for monitoring live store and ad data. Talonomic answers a narrower question with more depth: which SKUs actually make money — computed from your exports, with the formula shown for every number.

When Triple Whale or Polar is the better pick

  • You want real-time monitoring — hourly revenue, live ad performance, always-on dashboards.
  • Your main problem is ad attribution across channels rather than per-SKU profitability.
  • You prefer a connected pipeline over working from exports.

When Talonomic is the better pick

  • You need contribution margin and LTV per SKU, not blended storefront metrics.
  • You want to audit the math — every KPI ships with the formula that produced it.
  • You want drafted operator moves (reprice, cut freight, kill a SKU) that wait for your approval — recommendations, not auto-execution.
  • You'd rather start from an export in a minute than wire up connectors first.
